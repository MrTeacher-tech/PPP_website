How can we solve the problem of inequality withinin Califronia public education?

In order to solve this problem, we need to have more information on it. Luckily, all the data we need has already been collected, we just need it consolidated and analyzed.
The PPP is requesting that the California Department of Education and the California Department of Tax and Fee Administration create a database similar to the spreadsheet
we have made. This new database should display:
Total revenue for each school district
Revenue per student for each school district
Median income of households within each school district
Relvant student achievment scores for each district
Percent of students in district who attend UC's, CSU's or CCC's
