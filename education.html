Education

This table documents how much funding Bay Area school districts receive per student. Also noted is the student to teacher ratio of each district.
The majority of school revenue is generated by local property taxes. Thus, school districts in areas with high income families receive more funding
than districts whose families are of lower socioeconomic status. School funding has been proven to correlate to student achievement
(this is just one study of many). This creates what is known as "Social Reproduction".
